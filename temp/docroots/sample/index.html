Welcome to Contentment

Contentment is a content management system. Basically, that means it takes information (content) and helps to organize, update, and display it.

This is a sample web site implemented in Contentment to give you some pointers and help getting starting with Contentment. Contentment is still in the initial phases of development and this sample site is one of the last things to be put together. Thus, it's a bit sparse.
